FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this legal for customs and WeBOC submission?
Yes, absolutely. Our system generates documents in the standard formats accepted by Pakistan Customs and WeBOC. The documents are legally valid - our AI simply automates the data entry and formatting process that you would otherwise do manually. You remain responsible for the accuracy of the data you input.
What documents are supported?
We support all major export documents including: Commercial Invoices, Packing Lists, Certificates of Origin, Proforma Invoices, Shipping Instructions, WeBOC Goods Declaration (GD) data export, ANF-required documentation, and EFS scheme forms. We can also create custom templates for specific buyer requirements or destination countries.
How long does setup take?
Standard setup takes 2-3 business days. This includes configuring your company details, document templates, and initial training. For Enterprise plans with ERP integration, setup typically takes 1-2 weeks depending on the complexity of your existing systems.
Can it integrate with my existing ERP?
Yes! Our Business and Enterprise plans support integration with popular ERP systems including SAP, Oracle, Microsoft Dynamics, and custom-built solutions. We can also work with simpler systems like Excel-based order management through CSV uploads. Contact us for a custom integration assessment.
Do I need technical knowledge to use this?
Not at all. If you can use Excel, you can use our system. The interface is designed for export documentation staff - no IT background required. We provide training and ongoing support to ensure your team is comfortable with the system.
What happens if customs requirements change?
We actively monitor changes in Pakistan Customs regulations, WeBOC updates, and EFS scheme requirements. When changes occur, we update our templates and notify all customers. Monthly subscribers receive priority updates and template changes at no additional cost.

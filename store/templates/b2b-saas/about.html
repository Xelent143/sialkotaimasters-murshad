About Us

Building the Future of Business

From a small startup idea to a global enterprise platform, our journey has been defined by innovation, customer obsession, and a relentless pursuit of excellence.

Our Story

It Started With a Simple Question

In 2018, our founders asked: "Why is enterprise software so complicated?" They believed businesses deserved better—tools that were powerful yet intuitive, enterprise-grade yet accessible.

What began as a weekend project in a San Francisco garage has grown into a platform serving 500+ enterprises across 40 countries. But our mission remains unchanged: to democratize enterprise technology and empower businesses of all sizes to compete on a global scale.

  • Founded in 2018 in San Francisco
  • First customer signed within 3 months
  • Series B funding in 2022
  • Global expansion to 5 continents

2018 - 2024

The Beginning

From Humble Beginnings

Founded in 2016, Studio Vanguard began as a small team of three designers working out of a Brooklyn loft. We believed that great design could transform businesses, and we set out to prove it.

Today, we've grown into a full-service creative agency, but our core mission remains unchanged: to craft digital experiences that captivate, engage, and deliver real results for our clients.

Our approach combines strategic thinking with creative excellence. We don't just make things beautiful—we make them work. Every project begins with deep research and ends with measurable impact.

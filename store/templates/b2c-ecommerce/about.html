Our Story

From Dream to Reality

NOVA was founded in 2020 with a simple mission: to create fashion that empowers individuals to express their unique personalities. What started as a small studio in New York City has grown into a global brand, but our core values remain the same.

We believe that fashion is more than just clothing—it's a form of self-expression, a way to show the world who you are without saying a word. Every piece we create is designed with intention, crafted with care, and made to last.

Our team of designers draws inspiration from art, architecture, and the vibrant energy of city life to create collections that are both timeless and on-trend. We don't follow fashion—we define it.

Our Journey

The NOVA Story

2020

The Beginning

NOVA was founded in a small studio in New York City with just three employees and a dream.

2021

First Collection Launch

Our debut collection sold out within 48 hours, confirming our vision resonated with fashion lovers.

2022

Going Global

Expanded to international markets, opening our first flagship stores in London and Paris.

Michael Chen

Managing Partner & Chief Executive Officer

Harvard MBA CFA Charterholder McKinsey & Company

Michael founded Northgate Advisory in 2000 after 15 years at McKinsey & Company, where he led the global financial services practice. He has advised CEOs and boards of Fortune 500 companies across strategy, M&A, and transformation initiatives.

Under his leadership, Northgate has grown from a boutique consultancy to a global advisory firm with offices in New York, London, and Singapore. Michael specializes in strategic transformations and has led engagements that have created over $500M in shareholder value.

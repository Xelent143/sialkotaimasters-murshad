About Northgate Advisory

Twenty-five years of excellence in strategic consulting, driven by integrity, expertise, and an unwavering commitment to client success.

Our Story

From Vision to Excellence

Founded in 2000 by former McKinsey and BCG partners, Northgate Advisory began with a simple but ambitious mission: to bring Fortune 500-caliber strategic advisory to organizations of all sizes.

What started as a boutique consulting firm in New York City has grown into a global advisory practice with offices in London, Singapore, and Dubai. Yet despite our expansion, we've maintained the personalized approach and hands-on engagement that defined our early years.

Today, our team of 50+ consultants brings decades of collective experience from the world's leading consulting firms, investment banks, and corporate boardrooms. We've guided over 500 companies through transformative journeys, creating more than $2 billion in measurable value.

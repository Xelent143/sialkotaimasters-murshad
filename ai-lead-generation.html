FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this the official Lead Gen Pro API or unofficial?
We set up the official Lead Gen Pro API through Meta's verified partners. This means your business gets a verified profile (green tick possible), unlimited messaging capabilities, and zero risk of being banned. Unofficial WhatsApp automation tools can get your number blocked permanently.
Can I use my existing business phone number?
Yes, you can use your existing business number, but it will be migrated to the API and can't be used on regular WhatsApp anymore. We recommend using a dedicated business number for automation. We can help you get a new business SIM if needed.
How does bulk messaging work? Will customers get spammed?
Bulk messaging uses pre-approved message templates that customers opt into receiving. You can only message customers who have contacted you first or explicitly opted in. This keeps your messages compliant and welcomed by customers who want to hear from you.
Can customers still talk to a real person?
Absolutely! Automation handles repetitive tasks, but human agents can jump into any conversation at any time. We set up seamless handoff - customers can request to speak with a human, and the chatbot will notify your team immediately.
How long does setup take?
Starter packages are ready in 3-5 days. Business packages take 1-2 weeks. Enterprise solutions with custom integrations take 2-3 weeks. Meta's verification process (required for green tick) can take an additional 1-2 weeks.
